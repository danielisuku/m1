Mission statement

The aim of this website is to promote the music culture not just in Townsville but in Queensland as a whole. Our visuals will cut across a large age and taste demography and shed light into the various genres of music organised by our clients

Success Evaluation

The success of this website would be evaluated via the following

  • Amount of new members who volunteer for promoting concerts after site is lunched
  • Increase in ticket sales after lunch of website

Target Audience

  • Mature age demographic more likely between the ages of 24 – 45

We hope to make the site as simple possible while delivering the right and most important content and information without having to search through a lot of content
